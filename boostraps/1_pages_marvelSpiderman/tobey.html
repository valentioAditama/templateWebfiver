Tobias Vincent "Tobey" Maguire is an American actor who became famous when he was played as Peter Parker/Spider-Man in the Spider-Man films. He was born in Santa Monica, California. He has a career in film since 1989.

SPIDERMAN

Spider-Man is an American superhero film directed by Sam Raimi and written by David Koepp. Based on the fictional Marvel Comics character, Spider-Man Peter Parker is a weak teenager and is often bullied by his friends. But Peter also has friends, namely Gwen Stacy and Harry Osborn. Peter himself lived with his aunt, Aunt May, since his uncle died. Peter works as a freelance photographer in a newspaper publishing office called the daily bugle. Peter's beginning to become Spiderman was when he was on a study tour of one of the laboratories. and Peter was bitten by an irradiated spider. The spider itself detached and then bit the peter. Unwittingly, the spider's venom makes Peter have unexpected powers. After witnessing his uncle's death, Peter decides to use his newfound powers as Spider-Man to protect the city from evil.

SPIDERMAN 2

Peter Parker gave up on his life as a superhuman. He also decided to throw away his clothes to create a stir in society. Evil is rampant, so does Spiderman need to return to action? Meanwhile, Harry Osborn who has a grudge against Spiderman continues to find a way to kill the character nicknamed the spider man. On the same occasion, a scientist named Otto Octavius conducted an experiment with a mechanical hand that he created. He tried to create a new light source as an alternative energy from the sun. Unfortunately, the experiment failed and left Otto trapped in his mechanical hands. However, Otto still seems eager to make his dream come true. In line with Harry Osborn's evil efforts, he also asks Doctor Octopus to kill Spiderman with the promise of making ends meet. Drama and conflict ensued in this episode.

SPIDERMAN 3

Peter Parker's life as a human and a superhero is going well. His romance with Mary Jane went smoothly and Spider-Man was praised by many. Problems began to arise when Peter felt comfortable that Spider-Man was a figure loved by many. He even dared to kiss a woman in public which broke Mary Jane's heart. Moreover, photographer Eddie Brock, who later had the power of a symbiote, wanted revenge with Peter. He even invites Sandman to work together to defeat Peter.
